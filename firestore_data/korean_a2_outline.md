# Korean A2 Complete Module Summary

## Modules 3-12 (960 words)

### Module 3: Diary Form (반말) - 100 words
Plain/casual speech for friends and diary writing
- Verb stems + 아/어 endings
- Casual questions (뭐 해? 어디 가?)
- Casual responses (응, 아니)

### Module 4: Sentence Connectors Part 1 - 100 words
- 그래서 (so/therefore)
- 그러나 (however)
- 그런데 (but/by the way)
- 그리고 (and)
- 또한 (also)
- 게다가 (moreover)

### Module 5: Sentence Connectors Part 2 - 100 words
- 왜냐하면 (because)
- 따라서 (therefore)
- 그렇지만 (nevertheless)
- 그러므로 (thus)
- 즉 (namely)
- 예를 들어 (for example)

### Module 6: Intermediate Verbs - 100 words
Action verbs for TOPIK II level

### Module 7: Intermediate Adjectives - 100 words
Descriptive words for TOPIK II level

### Module 8: Social Situations - 100 words
Workplace, school, social interactions

### Module 9: Abstract Nouns - 100 words
Concepts, ideas, emotions

### Module 10: Compound Sentences - 100 words
Complex sentence structures

### Module 11: Idiomatic Expressions - 100 words
Common Korean idioms

### Module 12: TOPIK II Practice - 60 words
Reading comprehension vocabulary
